About Us


History and Background

"Artscape Studio & Gallery is an innovative venue that combines creativity, art, and interaction with the creative content community in Yogyakarta, Central Java.

Founded with the aim of being a hub for creativity, Artscape Studio & Gallery offers a dedicated space for content creators, artists, and art enthusiasts to collaborate, share ideas, and create inspiring works. It was founded by 5 DKV students of Satya Wacana Christian University."

Mission and Vision

Vision :
Providing a space for content creators and artists to collaborate and share ideas by facilitating content creation needs.

Mission :
Creating a quality and comfortable production site. Creating a gallery for artists to channel ideas, collaborate and become a place for promotion.
